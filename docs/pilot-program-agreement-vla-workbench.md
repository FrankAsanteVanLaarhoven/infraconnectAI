### 1. Pilot Program Overview

**Objective**:  
Allow Customer to evaluate the VLA Workbench platform in a real-world training environment to validate performance, ROI, and fit within Customer’s Physical AI workflows.

**Duration**: 60 calendar days from the Effective Date ("Pilot Period").

**Scope**:
- Access to full VLA Workbench (Professional tier features)
- Up to [X] parallel Isaac Lab environments
- Live data curation engine + 3D replay
- Dedicated onboarding and success support
- One production-like training project using Customer’s data (BDD100K, LIBERO, or custom)

### 2. Responsibilities

**Provider agrees to**:
- Provision the environment within 5 business days
- Provide training sessions (minimum 3)
- Offer priority support during the Pilot Period
- Deliver a final ROI and performance report at the end of the Pilot

**Customer agrees to**:
- Designate a technical point of contact
- Provide necessary data and environment access
- Participate in scheduled check-ins (bi-weekly)
- Provide feedback on usability and value

### 3. Data & Security

- All Customer data remains under Customer’s control and is processed only within the agreed environment.
- Provider will not use Customer data for training its own models.
- Full cryptographic audit logs will be provided at the end of the Pilot.
- Standard security and compliance terms from the Master Services Agreement apply.

---

### 4. Pilot Success Criteria

The Pilot will be considered successful if the following are met:

- Minimum **40%** reduction in wasted GPU hours (measured vs. baseline)
- Average Physics Score ≥ **0.82**
- Positive feedback from at least 3 engineers
- Successful deployment of at least one policy to hardware (if applicable)

### 5. Conversion & Pricing

At the end of the Pilot Period, Customer may:

- Convert to a standard annual subscription at the agreed rate, **or**
- Terminate with no further obligation

**Pilot Discount**: 50% off standard Professional or Enterprise rate for the 60-day period.
